Overview

Basis offers a foundation library for Scala that applies design thinking to the development of its code. The library strives to make it easy to do the right thing, without preventing you from doing what you want. It accomplishes this by extending simple interfaces outwardly with opt-in use-case driven extensions. This approach keeps the implementation focused, honest, and modular.

At the same time, brass tacks software engineering makes Basis fast and memory efficient. Clear design enables robust optimization without compromising usability. And use-directed development prudently in-sources many performance trade-offs.
